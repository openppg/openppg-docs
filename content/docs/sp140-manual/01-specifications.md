## Performance Characteristics

### Thrust & Power
- **Maximum Thrust**: 160 lbs (adjustable via controller)
- **Peak Power Output**: 20 kW continuous
- **Power System**: Brushless motor with FOC control
- **Efficiency**: Industry-leading power-to-weight ratio

### Flight Time
Flight duration varies based on:
- Battery capacity (2.6 kWh or 4.8 kWh)
- Pilot weight and gear
- Flying style and power settings
- Environmental conditions

**2.6 kWh Battery**: 20-40 minutes typical
**4.8 kWh Battery**: 55-80 minutes typical

### Weight & Balance
- **Frame Only**: 29 lbs
- **Small Battery (2.6 kWh)**: 25.8 lbs
- **Large Battery (4.8 kWh)**: 49.0 lbs
- **Total RTF Weight**: 54.1 lbs (small battery) to 78.4 lbs (large battery)

## Construction & Materials

### Frame
- **Primary Material**: 6061 T6 Aluminum
- **Structural Components**: 7075 T6 Aluminum
- **Fasteners**: Grade 5 Titanium
- **Spars**: Carbon Fiber
- **Netting**: Dyneema (5x stronger than steel)

### Propeller
- **Diameter**: 140 cm
- **Material**: Carbon Fiber
- **Options**: Bi-blade or Tri-blade configurations
- **Quick Release**: Tool-free hub system

## Electrical System

### Motor
- **Type**: Custom OpenPPG v2.5 brushless outrunner
- **Power Rating**: 25 kW continuous
- **Efficiency**: High-efficiency design
- **Cooling**: Integrated airflow cooling

### Motor Controller (ESC)
- **Control Method**: Field-Oriented Control (FOC)
- **Weather Protection**: Weatherproof design
- **Communication**: CanBus controlled
- **Safety Features**: Multiple redundant protections

### Hand Controller
- **Display**: Color LCD with real-time telemetry
- **Features**:
  - Built-in altimeter
  - Haptic feedback
  - EICAS (Engine Indicating and Crew Alerting System)
  - GPS tracking
  - Bluetooth connectivity

### Battery Pack
- **Chemistry**: High energy density MNC cells
- **Management**: Built-in Bluetooth BMS
- **Communication**: CanBus integration
- **Capacities**: 2.6 kWh or 4.8 kWh options
- **Design**: Custom paramotor-specific packaging

## Operating Conditions

### Temperature Range
- **Standard Operation**: -10°C to 40°C
- **Storage**: 10°C to 25°C recommended
- **Charging**: Follow manufacturer guidelines

### Environmental Considerations
- Weatherproof motor controller
- Sealed battery enclosure
- Protected electronics
- Cold weather capability (see [After-Flight Care](../09-after-flight))

## Warranty
- **Duration**: 12 months or 300 hours of operation (whichever comes first)
- **Coverage**: Comprehensive parts and labor
- **Details**: See [Warranty Information](../10-warranty)
