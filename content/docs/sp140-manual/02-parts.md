## Power System Layout

![Power Pack](images/image29.png)

The power system consists of:

1. **Motor** - Mounted at the front on the motor mount plate
2. **ESC** - Mounted below the motor
3. **Battery** - Slides into frame from bottom
4. **Hand Controller** - Connects via cable to ESC
5. **BMS Connection** - Data cable from battery to ESC

## Important Identification Notes

- **Hoop Connectors**: Numbered 0-6, must be installed in correct order
- **CF Spars**: Two shorter spars for bottom positions
- **Battery Orientation**: Note "THIS SIDE UP" marking
- **Propeller Direction**: Check rotation arrow on prop

## Parts List Verification

When unboxing, verify you have received:

**Frame Components**:
- Center frame assembly
- 2x Center frame legs
- 6x Frame hoop sections
- 7x Hoop connectors (numbered 0-6)
- 6x Carbon fiber spars (4 long, 2 short)
- Netting with rods
- 2x Goose neck bars
- Harness

**Power System**:
- Motor with mount plate
- Motor controller (ESC)
- Hand controller
- Battery pack (2.6 or 4.8 kWh)
- Charger
- Propeller with quick-release hub
- All mounting hardware

**Hardware & Tools**:
- Allen keys
- Socket wrench
- Titanium bolts and lock nuts
- Instruction manual

If any parts are missing or damaged, contact info@openppg.com with your order details.
