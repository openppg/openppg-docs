## Support Services

### General Support

For general questions and support:
- **Email**: [info@openppg.com](mailto:info@openppg.com)
- **Response Time**: 1-2 business days
- **Hours**: Business hours, US time zones

For technical issues:
- Start with [troubleshooting guides](../05-startup)
- Check [alerts documentation](../06-alerts)
- Review [community forum](https://community.openppg.com) for similar issues

- Subject: "Warranty Claim - [Order Number]"

For order status or returns:
- Email: [info@openppg.com](mailto:info@openppg.com)
- **Always include your order number**
- Provide order date and details
- Describe issue clearly

## Getting Help

### Before Contacting Support

1. **Check this manual** for relevant information
2. **Search the forum** for similar issues
3. **Review video tutorials** on YouTube
4. **Try basic troubleshooting**:
   - Power cycle the system
   - Check connections
   - Verify battery charge
   - Look for error codes

### When Contacting Support

Provide:
- **Order number** and purchase date
- **Description of issue** (detailed)
- **Photos or videos** if applicable
- **Error codes** from hand controller
- **Flight hours** from app
- **Steps already taken** to resolve
- **System version** (firmware/software)

### Emergency Support

For critical safety issues:
- Email immediately: [info@openppg.com](mailto:info@openppg.com)
- Subject: "URGENT - Safety Issue"
- Do not fly until resolved
- Await response before operation
